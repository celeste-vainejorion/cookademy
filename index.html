What is Cookademy?

Cookademy offers immersive cooking workshops where you’ll learn to prepare local specialties with passionate chefs. Whether you're a total beginner or a curious foodie, our sessions are designed to inspire your inner cook and bring you closer to the cultures of Lyon, Rome and Barcelona.
